A Tuscan Dream Wedding in Villa San Martino

Where love meets luxury in the rolling hills of Italy

When Sarah and Michael first reached out to us, they had a vision that seemed straight out of a fairy tale. They dreamed of exchanging vows in the heart of Tuscany, surrounded by rolling vineyards, ancient olive groves, and the timeless beauty that only Italy can offer. What unfolded at Villa San Martino was nothing short of magical â€“ a celebration that perfectly captured their love story while embracing the romantic spirit of this enchanting region.

The journey began over a year ago when Sarah, an art curator from New York, and Michael, a sommelier with a passion for Italian wines, decided they wanted their wedding to reflect their shared love for art, culture, and fine wine. They had visited Tuscany during their engagement and fell in love with Villa San Martino's 16th-century architecture and breathtaking panoramic views.
